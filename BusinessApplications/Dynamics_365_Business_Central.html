What is Dynamics 365 Business Central?

Dynamics 365 Business Central is a cloud-based enterprise resource planning (ERP) solution from Microsoft, designed for small to medium-sized businesses.

Key Features:

  • Financial management (general ledger, budgeting, reporting)
  • Inventory and supply chain management
  • Sales and customer relationship tracking
  • Project and job management
  • Light manufacturing and warehouse operations
  • Integration with Microsoft 365, Power BI, and other tools

Cloud-Based & Scalable

Business Central runs on Microsoft Azure and is delivered as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution, allowing secure, scalable access from anywhere.

Part of the Dynamics 365 Family

Business Central is part of the broader Dynamics 365 suite of business applications and is categorized under Business Applications.

How to See Your Microsoft 365 & Power Platform Account Info

  • Users & Licenses: Log in to admin.microsoft.com to view all users, reset passwords, and manage licenses.
  • Power Platform Environments: Go to admin.powerplatform.microsoft.com to see your Power Apps environments and settings.
  • Developer Program: Check your E5 subscription and sample data at developer.microsoft.com.
  • Full Directory Info: Visit entra.microsoft.com to see all users, groups, and roles in your Azure AD / Entra ID.
Summary: Use these portals to manage users, licenses, environments, and your developer account. Everything is accessible with your Microsoft 365 admin login.
