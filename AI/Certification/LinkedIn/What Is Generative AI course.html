Course Overview

Whether you work in film, marketing, healthcare, automobile, or real estate, generative AI is changing the way your job is executed. Those who adapt early will reap its benefits sooner. All professions will be affected by generative AI, which is considered a creative revolution comparable to the invention of photography. This course, led by generative AI expert Pinar Seyhan Demirdag, covers the basics of generative AI, including what it is, how it works, how to create your own content, different types of models, future predictions, and ethical implications.

This course is part of a Professional Certificate from Microsoft.

Learning Objectives

  • Differentiate between AI and the subcategories of AI.
  • Determine the elements involved when a user creates their own content with AI.
  • Describe the functionality of natural language models.
  • Explain how variational autoencoders (VAEs) are used in anomaly detection.
  • Articulate what executives and leaders should implement with AI.

Skills Covered

  • Content creation with AI
  • AI in business and leadership
  • Ethical implications of AI
