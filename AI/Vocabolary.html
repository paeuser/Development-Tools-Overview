AI Vocabulary

Llama
An open-source large language model (LLM) developed by Meta, used for natural language processing tasks such as text generation, summarization, and question answering.
Mistral
A family of open-source large language models (LLMs) known for efficiency and strong performance, often used for generative AI tasks.
Diffusion models
Generative models that create data (such as images) by iteratively denoising random noise, widely used in state-of-the-art AI image generation (e.g., Stable Diffusion).
RAGs (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
AI systems that combine retrieval of relevant documents or data with generative models to produce more accurate and context-aware responses.
Vive coding
Likely refers to hands-on, interactive coding or live coding sessions, often used for learning or demonstrating AI concepts in real time.
Andrew Ng
A leading AI researcher, educator, and co-founder of Google Brain and Coursera, known for making AI and machine learning accessible to a broad audience.
Andrej Karpathy
Renowned AI researcher and educator, former Director of AI at Tesla, and a key contributor to deep learning and large language models.
