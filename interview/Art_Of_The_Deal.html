Harvey Mackay – Business Author

"You don’t get what you deserve. You get what you negotiate."

What pay do you expect?

I’m still learning about the role, so it would be helpful to know the salary range you’re offering. That way, we can make sure we’re on the same page before moving forward.

I have done some research on the position, but I don’t know what the salary band is for this role. Could you share that with me?

Thanks for reaching out and sharing the details! I’m sure we can work something out that’s in line with the market range for this kind of opportunity. If you have a range in mind, feel free to share it—this way, we can ensure we’re aligned before moving ahead.

Questions to Ask During the Interview

  • What are the key responsibilities of this role?
  • What qualities or skills are you looking for in the ideal candidate?
  • Are there any gaps or challenges the team is currently facing?
  • What is the “missing puzzle piece” you hope to find with this hire?
  • What are the top priorities for the person in this position?

Interview Tips

  • Show that you pay attention to details.
  • Research the company culture.
