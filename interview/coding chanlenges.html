Coding Challenges & System Design Interview Prep

Popular Coding Challenge Platforms

  • Codility — Practice timed coding tests, often used by employers for screening. You can practice with their demo tasks and challenges.
  • HackerRank — Practice coding problems, data structures, algorithms, and take company-specific challenges. Free to practice and track your progress.
  • LeetCode — Extensive library of coding problems, mock interviews, and company-specific questions. Free tier available; premium unlocks more features. Great for practicing coding interviews.

System Design Interview Resources

  • System Design Primer (GitHub) — Comprehensive guide to system design concepts, sample questions, and scalable architecture patterns. Free and open source. No interactive practice, but excellent for study and reference.

What to Expect

  • You may be asked to solve live coding problems on platforms like Codility or HackerRank during interviews.
  • For system design interviews, you may be asked to design scalable systems, discuss trade-offs, and explain your architecture.

Tips

  • Practice coding problems regularly on LeetCode, HackerRank, or Codility to build speed and confidence.
  • Review the System Design Primer to prepare for architecture and design questions.
  • Many platforms allow you to practice for free. LeetCode and HackerRank have large communities and discussion forums.
