Assurity Life Insurance Company

Assurity Life Insurance Company does not offer traditional major medical health insurance plans. Instead, they provide supplemental health insurance products designed to complement existing health coverage.

  • Critical Illness Insurance: Provides a lump-sum cash benefit upon diagnosis of covered serious illnesses, such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke.
  • Disability Income Insurance: Offers monthly benefits to replace a portion of income if you're unable to work due to illness or injury.
  • Accident Insurance: Pays benefits for injuries resulting from accidents, helping to cover unexpected medical costs.
  • Hospital Indemnity Insurance: Provides benefits to help cover out-of-pocket expenses and associated fees for hospital stays.

Note: Assurity's products are designed to supplement, not replace, major medical insurance.
